About me

I have a degree in Industrial Organizational Psychology, which is to say “business psych”. That degree taught me the humanistic, yet numerical, thinking which would become the foundation to all of my art. For instance, I’ve been working on a Sudoku app that’s taken about a year and a half now. In particular, I’m most proud of the tutorial I put together. There, I spent a lot of time, thought, and iterating into making it easy to understand, so someone who has never played Sudoku can pick it up immediately. That’s the type of work that makes me excited to be a UX / UI professional: conducting research, learning about a product, what users want in the end, what actually looks good, and mixing it all together in a careful way. In general, I believe taking a user experience approach to designs is something we could all benefit from. Thank you to the person reading all this, I hope I was able to paint a picture of who I am as an artist and what I stand for in the world of design!
